Chennai Volunteer Taskforce

Coordinated by Chennai Tricolor Initiative

We are coordinating across various NGOs, government officials, and citizens to help fight the biggest crisis we have faced since Indian Independence. 

Nine Volunteer Taskforces

COVID Emergency Asks

Focusing on streamlining demands for Beds, Oxygen and other asks (food, financial help etc)

COVID Information Portal

Helps Chennai with credible and verified information during COVID19. Updated everyday

Visit: https://chennaicovidhelp.in/

COVID Vaccination Drive

The Volunteer Taskforce to help achieve 40 Lakh vaccinations in 30 days in Chennai.

COVID Food Delivery

We help list, verify and coordinate both paid for as well as donated meals. Willing to deliver parcels when required.

Visit: https://chennaicovidhelp.in/ (Food Delivery)

COVID Infra Upgrade

Focus of Group : to increase capacity and to improve processes within healthcare infra.

COVID #MaskUp Campaign

Team working on the #Maskup campaign with the goal of improving mask adherance from 70% to 95%

COVID Child Care

To create awareness and provide resources to 10,000 families within 2 months.

COVID Tech Interventions

Tech volunteers working on several solutions to help flatten the curve.

COVID Samaritan

To provide financial assistance.

200+ Volunteers

From across various disciplines who are working towards the same goal. We’ve divided our group into 9 task forces and everything is run on either WhatsApp. Each group has one or two owners but it is extremely democratic and anyone can jump in and work where they feel they can add value.
