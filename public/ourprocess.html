Our Process

1

Step 1

Once we get the request, our volunteer team will review the same.
2

Step 2

If hospitalisation is required, the request is assigned to one of our volunteers.
3

Step 3

Our volunteers use bed status data from several sources, and call hospitals directly. 
4

Step 4

You can track the status of your request through the Track Status page. 
5

Step 5

Once the bed is available, we will inform you. If not, we will keep trying.

We are sorry you are in this situation.

We sincerely hope we are able to find you a bed for your family or friend.
And most importantly they fight through this battle and come out stronger.
